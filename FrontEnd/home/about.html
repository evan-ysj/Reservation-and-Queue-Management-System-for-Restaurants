Welcome to Taste of Italy A Restaurant

Our restaurant is the premier Italian restaurant in Toronto. We offer most authentic Italian experience from the moment our guests arrive. Our restaurant is a place where our guests can create memories that last a lifetime. Whether you need the perfect setting for a romantic dinner, business meeting, or special function, we can cater to your every need and give you the dining experience you and your guests will crave time and again. During the warmer months, our patio gives our guests the perfect opportunity to enjoy the weather with a delicious meal and a cold beverage with friends and loved ones. Our menu boasts authentic Italian dishes, many of which can be traced back to the ancestors of our family owned and operated establishment. If you want an authentic Italian dining experience, our restaurant is your best choice. Visit us today and see why Taste of Italy is the most popular choice among diners in Toronto.

Our Team

THIS IS THE DREAM TEAM, PAST AND PRESENT. WITHOUT THEM, THIS DREAM WOULD NOT BE POSSIBLE.

Victor Barry

Chef

Victor is a born and bred Ontario chef. His work ethic, passed down from his father, has driven him to his position as one of Toronto’s premier chefs. He has honed his culinary skills around the world and has made an indelible mark on Toronto’s restaurant landscape.

Kyle Rindinella

Chef

Kyle grew up just outside of Milan, where his favourite childhood memories involved weekend ski trips to Switzerland, France and Germany. Although he moved to Brampton, ON at the age of 11 he has always valued his time growing up in Italy and believes that time shaped much of who he is today.

Patrick Jacobson

Senior Chef

As Chef-Owner of Taste of Italy Restaurant, Patrick is once again forging new ground on the culinary scene. While he remains focused on finding the magic inherent in every single ingredient; he is allowing himself and his guests a chance to slow down and savour experiences that come with great food, great wine, and each other.

Ivan Dorchsner

Chef Assistant

Ivan’s desire to give his guests a singular culinary experience in Toronto will never change, though the way he does that has. At Taste of Italy Restaurant, Ivan and his family welcome you to their ideal Italian restaurant, where your hunger is sated and soul is nourished.
